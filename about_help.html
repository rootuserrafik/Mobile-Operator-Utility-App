How to Use This App

Welcome to Operator App! This app is designed to make managing your mobile services quick and easy.

- **Home:** Check your balance and access key features with one tap.
- **Offers:** Browse and activate the latest internet, minute, and combo packs.
- **Dial Codes:** Quickly find and dial important USSD codes for various services.

Use the navigation bar at the bottom to switch between pages. For any issues, please contact our support team.
